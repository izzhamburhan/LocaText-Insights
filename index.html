User Support

Welcome to our support center. Here, you can find answers to common questions and get assistance.

How can I interpret the Malaysia Map in the Main Dashboard?

The Malaysia Map in the Main Dashboard provides a geographical overview of the dataset. Each marked location on the map represents regions with varying activity levels. The color indicates the interest with the most posts for each state.

What insights can I gain from the Word Cloud Overall in the Main Dashboard?

The Word Cloud Overall visually summarizes the most frequently occurring words across all interests in the dataset. Larger text indicates higher frequency, offering a quick snapshot of popular words discussed in the posts.

How does the Heatmap and Bar Chart in the Interest Page work?

The Heatmap and Bar Chart in the Interest Page showcase the intensity of text values across locations for various interests. The Heatmap visually represents text intensity with distinct colors for each interest, while the Bar Chart illustrates interest distribution across different locations.

What is the purpose of the Navigation Section in the Interest Page?

The Navigation Section consists of buttons corresponding to different interests. Users can click on each button to navigate to a dedicated page providing detailed information for a specific interest, allowing deeper exploration.

How is the Two Experiment Comparison in the Experiment Result Page useful?

The Two Experiment Comparison in the Experiment Result Page presents a horizontal bar chart comparing the performance of different machine learning classifiers. This chart offers a quick overview of how each classifier excels in specific interest categories, helping users make informed decisions.
